# P1.3 — SAT-core modernization

**Size:** M–L · **Depends on:** [P1.1](P1.1-sat-inprocessing.md) (do the big
inprocessing wins first) · **Front:** performance

## Goal
Bring axeyum's custom CDCL core (`axeyum-cnf`) up to modern competitive design:
branching mode switching, EMA/Luby restarts, phase saving/rephasing, a cache-tight
arena clause DB with packed watches, and chronological backtracking.

## Why / leverage
After preprocessing (P1.2) and inprocessing (P1.1) shrink the problem, the search
loop's constant factors and heuristics decide the remaining gap to Z3. These are
well-understood, individually measurable knobs. Per the methodology gate, the
arena/watch redesign is deferred until propagation is proven the bottleneck.

## Phase exit criteria
- Phase saving + EMA restarts + mode switching + tiered reduction land and each is
  measured on the committed slice (recorded), with net PAR-2 improvement.
- The arena/watch redesign (if done) keeps the workspace `unsafe`-free and
  deterministic; binary-clause and blocking-literal fast paths are present.
- Differential-vs-Z3 agreement unchanged.
