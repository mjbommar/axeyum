# P4.4 — SMT-LIB command-surface completeness

**Size:** M · **Depends on:** — (independent; `get-proof` ties to
[Track 3](../track-3-proof-lean/README.md))

## Goal
Fill the SMT-LIB 2 command-surface gaps so axeyum is a drop-in front end for
tooling that drives Z3/cvc5: parametric sorts, reset, full option handling,
assignments, and `get-proof`.

## Why / leverage
Mechanical but broad — it's what lets external tools (and benchmark drivers) use
axeyum without special-casing. `get-proof` also surfaces the Track 3 proof work to
the command line.
