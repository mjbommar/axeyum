# P2.8 — FP semantic repair and completeness

**Size:** S–M · **Depends on:** — (independent)

## Goal
Restore a sound SMT-LIB floating-point semantic boundary, then measure and close
the remaining completeness gaps. Axeyum has broad F16–F128 operator coverage,
but the 2026-07-22 audit found quotient-domain, rounding-mode, underspecified-
result, directed-overflow, and Float128 defects. This is a semantic repair and
assurance phase, not evidence of general solver parity.
