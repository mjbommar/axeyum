# Complex Plane Transforms V0

This pack extends the `complex_analysis` field with exact finite checks on the
complex plane. It builds on `complex-algebraic-v0`: complex numbers are still
real-pair rational values, but the examples now look more like the objects a
learner sees in complex analysis.

The pack covers:

- the order-four unit-root cycle generated by `i`;
- conjugation of a fixed product;
- a rational Mobius-transform witness;
- checked rejection of a false claim about squares of unit complex numbers;
- a Lean-horizon row for holomorphic and contour-integral theorems.

## Trust Story

The validator parses every real and imaginary component as an exact rational
string. It recomputes powers, products, conjugates, division, denominator
norms, and the explicit counterexample without floating point.

This is still a finite algebraic replay pack. It does not prove Cauchy-Riemann
equations, Cauchy's theorem, residues, analytic continuation, or the
fundamental theorem of algebra.
