# Checks

## `finite-topology-axioms`

Expected result: `sat`.

The validator checks that the listed family of open sets is a topology over the
finite universe.

## `closure-interior-witness`

Expected result: `sat`.

The validator recomputes interior and closure for the fixed subset `{b}`.

## `metric-ball-witness`

Expected result: `sat`.

The validator checks a finite rational metric table and recomputes the open
ball centered at `p1` with radius `3/2`.
